## Pinout Safety Rule

Always verify pinout before use. Never assume balanced adapters are safe across systems.

### Common 4.4 mm TRRRS Balanced Pinout

- Tip: L+
- Ring 1: L-
- Ring 2: R+
- Ring 3: R-
- Sleeve: Ground / shield, if used

### Dual 3-pin XLR Headphone Wiring

Typical headphone balanced wiring:

- Left XLR pin 2: L+
- Left XLR pin 3: L-
- Right XLR pin 2: R+
- Right XLR pin 3: R-
- Pin 1: shield/ground depending on cable design

## Required Checks

- Is the adapter for headphone output or line-level XLR?
- Male/female direction
- Pinout published by seller
- Whether pin 1 is connected
- Whether 4.4 sleeve is tied to XLR pin 1
- Whether the amp/source allows shared ground or shield connection
