# Audeze LCD-3 vs Audeze LCD-5s

## Short Verdict

For Vincent's collection and listening priorities, LCD-3 remains the more attractive long-term used-market target. LCD-5s is technically newer and likely more resolving, but LCD-3 offers stronger classic status, used-market value, and system complementarity.

## Positioning

| Model | Position | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| LCD-3 | Classic musical Audeze flagship | Warmth, density, vocals, rock, prog, melodic metal |
| LCD-5s | Modern reference flagship | Resolution, speed, accuracy, monitoring-style clarity |

## Why LCD-3 Remains Higher Priority

- Better used-market value around $900-$1,000.
- Stronger established Head-Fi / collector history.
- Richer and more organic tonal identity.
- Better complement to Vincent's HD800-based system.
- More emotionally satisfying for Pink Floyd, melodic death metal, progressive metal, and vocal-oriented listening.

## Why LCD-5s Still Matters

- Newer Audeze technology platform.
- More reference-oriented tuning.
- Likely superior technical resolution and transient precision.
- Potentially better for critical listening and studio-style analysis.

## Buying Recommendation

### Choose LCD-3 if:

- Price is <= $1,000.
- Official driver replacement/new-driver history is documented.
- You want warmth, body, and a classic Audeze identity.
- You want a strong complement to HD800 and Luxman P-1u.

### Choose LCD-5s if:

- You specifically want Audeze's latest technical platform.
- You prefer a more reference/monitoring direction.
- Used prices drop substantially after the initial launch period.
- You already own LCD-3 or do not care about used-market value.
