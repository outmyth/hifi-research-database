# Sennheiser IE900 vs DUNU Falcon Ultra

## Short Verdict

IE900 is the technical flagship IEM reference. Falcon Ultra should be treated as the practical dynamic-driver contrast: potentially warmer, easier, and more value-oriented, but not a replacement for IE900's resolution and stage.

## Positioning

| Model | Role | Best Reason to Use |
|---|---|---|
| IE900 | Technical flagship DD IEM | Resolution, stage, speed, flagship detail |
| Falcon Ultra | Practical/value DD IEM | Natural tone, value, easier listening contrast |

## Decision Logic

Choose IE900 when:

- You want maximum technical performance.
- You are listening to progressive or technical metal.
- You have a warm enough source/tip combination.

Choose Falcon Ultra when:

- IE900 sounds too thin or metallic.
- You want easier long listening.
- You want a lower-risk everyday dynamic-driver IEM.

## Vincent Notes

- Test both from Sony WM1ZM2 first.
- Then test LPG 2017 for speed/directness.
