# Koss ESP/950 vs Stax SR-007 Mk1

## Short Verdict

ESP/950 is the low-cost classic electrostatic flavor. SR-007 Mk1 is the serious high-end electrostatic system project. They are not direct substitutes: ESP/950 is a bargain collector piece, while SR-007 Mk1 requires a full Stax amplification strategy.

## Positioning

| Model | Role | Best Reason to Buy |
|---|---|---|
| Koss ESP/950 | Affordable complete electrostatic classic | Value and simplicity around $300-$350 |
| Stax SR-007 Mk1 | Serious flagship electrostatic classic | Long-term Stax reference and collector status |

## Sound / System Difference

| Area | ESP/950 | SR-007 Mk1 |
|---|---|---|
| System cost | Low if complete | High after amp/energizer included |
| Bass | Light, clean | More substantial if properly driven |
| Midrange | Smooth and easy | Denser, more refined, more serious |
| Treble | Airy but gentle | Extended and refined |
| Dynamics | Limited macrodynamic slam | Much more capable with proper amp |
| Collector tier | High value classic | True flagship classic |
| Ownership risk | Moderate | Higher due to amp/cable/driver/system dependence |

## Buying Logic

### Choose ESP/950 if:

- Complete system is <= $350.
- You want electrostatic flavor without building a Stax system.
- You want a low-risk collectible.
- You already own ESP/95X but find a true ESP/950 at a strong price.

### Choose SR-007 Mk1 if:

- Price is <= $1,500 and condition is verified.
- You are ready to plan the amplifier/energizer side properly.
- You want a serious electrostatic reference.
- You are comfortable with higher ownership risk and cost.

## Vincent Decision Rule

- ESP/950 around $300: buy as collector flavor.
- SR-007 Mk1 around $1,500: research seriously, but only with amp plan.
- For immediate metal value: LCD-X and LCD-3 remain more practical.
- For long-term electrostatic summit: SR-007 Mk1 outranks ESP/950.

## Notes

This comparison separates "cheap classic electrostatic" from "serious Stax system project." They should not compete in the same budget category.
