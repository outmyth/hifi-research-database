# Audeze LCD-X vs Ultrasone Pro 900 for Metal

## Short Verdict

LCD-X is the stronger all-around metal headphone, especially for technical death metal and progressive metal. Pro 900 is the more aggressive, fun, V-shaped specialist and is attractive mainly because it offers a very different flavor at a low used price.

## Positioning

| Model | Role | Best At |
|---|---|---|
| Audeze LCD-X | Serious planar metal/reference choice | Speed, control, separation, bass discipline |
| Ultrasone Pro 900 | Fun closed-back metal specialist | Bass punch, V-shaped aggression, old Head-Fi flavor |

## Metal Subgenre Fit

| Subgenre | LCD-X | Pro 900 | Notes |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Death Metal | 5/5 | 4/5 | LCD-X is cleaner and more controlled. |
| Technical Death Metal | 5/5 | 3/5 | LCD-X handles complexity better. |
| Melodic Death Metal | 4/5 | 4/5 | LCD-X more balanced; Pro 900 more exciting. |
| Power Metal | 4/5 | 4/5 | Pro 900 can be fun, LCD-X is more accurate. |
| Progressive Metal | 5/5 | 3/5 | LCD-X is clearly better for complex mixes. |
| Symphonic / Gothic Metal | 4/5 | 3/5 | LCD-X has better layering; Pro 900 may be too V-shaped. |

## Why Choose LCD-X

- Faster and more controlled planar bass.
- Better separation for dense metal arrangements.
- More balanced tuning.
- Better long-term serious listening choice.
- Better complement if Vincent wants a modern Audeze metal tool.

## Why Choose Pro 900

- Much cheaper target price.
- Closed-back and very different from HD800/Audeze/electrostatic systems.
- Strong fun factor.
- Historical Head-Fi cult appeal.
- Great if the goal is energy rather than accuracy.

## Buying Logic

### LCD-X Buy Zone

- 2021+ version around $600-$650 is very attractive.
- Above $750, wait unless condition/accessories are excellent.

### Pro 900 Buy Zone

- First-generation Pro 900 <= $180 is attractive.
- Around $160 is a strong buy if clean.
- Avoid high prices based on old MSRP.

## Vincent Decision

- If choosing only one metal workhorse: LCD-X.
- If choosing a cheap specialist flavor: Pro 900.
- If LCD-3 is already acquired, LCD-X becomes less urgent unless a very strong deal appears.
- Pro 900 remains useful because it does not overlap much with LCD-3 or HD800.
