# Audeze LCD-2C vs LCD-3 for Vincent

## Short Verdict

LCD-2C is a strong value Audeze when very cheap, but LCD-3 is the better long-term match for Vincent's collection, music taste, and existing HD800/Luxman system. LCD-2C should not distract from a clean LCD-3 at a good price.

## Positioning

| Model | Role | Best Reason to Buy |
|---|---|---|
| LCD-2C | Affordable dark Audeze flavor | Low-cost planar warmth |
| LCD-3 | Classic flagship Audeze | Rich mids, collector value, emotional long-term ownership |

## Sound Comparison

| Area | LCD-2C | LCD-3 |
|---|---|---|
| Bass | Full and dark | Fuller, more refined, better layered |
| Midrange | Warm but simpler | Lush, dense, liquid |
| Treble | Dark and forgiving | Smooth but more refined |
| Speed | Solid but relaxed | More resolving and nuanced |
| Stage | Moderate | More immersive and premium |
| Overall | Value Audeze | Classic high-end Audeze |

| Genre | LCD-2C | LCD-3 | Notes |
|---|---:|---:|---|

## Buying Logic

### Choose LCD-2C if:

- Price is around $350-$400.
- It is clean, recent, and includes case/cable.
- You want a low-risk introduction to Audeze planar warmth.
- LCD-3 is not available near target price.

### Choose LCD-3 if:

- Price is <= $1,000.
- It has official driver service/new-driver proof.
- You want a true long-term collector piece.
- You want a strong complement to HD800 rather than just a cheaper planar flavor.

## Vincent Decision Rule

- LCD-2C at $400: good value, but not a must-buy.
- LCD-3 at $900-$1,000: higher priority.
- LCD-2C above $500: usually pass and save for LCD-3 or LCD-X.
- If the collection goal matters, LCD-3 wins.

## Notes

LCD-2C is attractive only as a price-driven value buy. LCD-3 is the emotional and collector target.
