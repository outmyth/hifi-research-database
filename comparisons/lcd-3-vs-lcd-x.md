# Audeze LCD-3 vs LCD-X

## Short Verdict

LCD-3 is the more collectible, emotional, and system-complementary Audeze for Vincent. LCD-X is the more practical metal/workhorse Audeze, especially in the 2021+ revision. The correct choice depends on whether the goal is long-term classic ownership or metal-focused speed and control.

## Positioning

| Model | Role | Best Reason to Buy |
|---|---|---|
| LCD-3 | Classic musical Audeze flagship | Richness, mids, atmosphere, collector value |
| LCD-X | Modern practical Audeze workhorse | Speed, neutrality, metal performance, value |

## Sound Comparison

| Area | LCD-3 | LCD-X |
|---|---|---|
| Bass | Fuller, warmer, more enveloping | Tighter, faster, more controlled |
| Midrange | Lush, dense, emotional | More neutral and direct |
| Treble | Smooth and relaxed | More energetic and revealing |
| Speed | Good but lush | Faster and more precise |
| Stage | Immersive and thick | Cleaner and more studio-like |
| Tone | Romantic/classic | Practical/reference |

## Buying Logic

### Choose LCD-3 if:

- Price is <= $1,000.
- It has recent official driver replacement or 2021/new-driver documentation.
- You want long-term collector value.
- You want a warm, rich complement to HD800.

### Choose LCD-X if:

- 2021+ version appears around $600-$650.
- You want one of the best Audeze options for metal speed and control.
- You want better practical value and lower ownership cost.
- You do not need the LCD-3's lush collector tone.

## Vincent Decision Rule

- If LCD-3 is $900-$1,000 and healthy: prioritize LCD-3.
- If LCD-X 2021+ is $600-$650 and LCD-3 is unavailable/overpriced: LCD-X is excellent.
- If both appear at strong prices, LCD-3 is the collection pick, LCD-X is the metal workhorse pick.
- If only one Audeze can be kept long term for Vincent's broader taste: LCD-3.
