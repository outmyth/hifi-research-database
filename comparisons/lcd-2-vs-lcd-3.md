# Audeze LCD-2 vs LCD-3

## Short Verdict

LCD-2 is the value/classic Audeze entry, while LCD-3 is the higher-priority long-term collector and listening target for Vincent. If prices are close, choose LCD-3. LCD-2 only wins when the deal is much cheaper or a desirable revision appears.

## Positioning

| Model | Role | Best Reason to Buy |
|---|---|---|
| LCD-2 | Foundational Audeze warmth | Value, classic planar tone, lower cost |
| LCD-3 | Higher-end classic Audeze flagship | Richer mids, better collector value, stronger long-term target |

## Sound Comparison

| Area | LCD-2 | LCD-3 |
|---|---|---|
| Bass | Thick and warm | Fuller, more refined, more layered |
| Midrange | Warm and pleasant | More liquid, dense, and emotional |
| Treble | Smooth/dark | Smooth but generally more refined |
| Speed | Moderate | Better, though still lush |
| Stage | Intimate | More layered and immersive |
| Overall | Classic value Audeze | More complete musical flagship |

## Buying Logic

### Choose LCD-2 if:

- Price is around $450-$500.
- It is a desirable revision such as Rev.2.
- Condition is excellent and driver health is confirmed.
- You want Audeze warmth at lower cost.

### Choose LCD-3 if:

- Price is <= $1,000.
- It has official driver replacement/new-driver history.
- You want the more collectible and emotionally complete Audeze.
- You want a long-term complement to HD800.

## Vincent Decision Rule

- LCD-2 at $500 can be good.
- LCD-3 at $900-$1,000 is better for the long-term collection.
- Do not pay high LCD-2 prices that approach discounted LCD-3 territory.
