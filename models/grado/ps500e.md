# Grado PS500e

## Summary

A compact metal-cup Grado owned by Vincent, useful as a more grounded and slightly warmer Grado flavor than some brighter wooden models. It is a strong vocal/rock reference and a good practical Grado.

## Status

- Category: Open-back dynamic headphone
- Collector tier: A

- Priority: Keep as practical Grado reference

## Ownership Notes

- Purchased locally around 2026-04 for approximately $320 cash in Sunnyvale.
- No original box noted.
- Used mainly for vocals.
- Easy to drive.

## Sound Signature

### Bass

Punchier and fuller than many classic Grados, though not a deep-bass specialist.

### Midrange

Forward and engaging, with strong vocal focus.

### Treble

Energetic but often less sharp than some leaner Grado presentations.

### Soundstage / Imaging

Intimate Grado-style stage.

### Dynamics / Speed

Lively, direct, and easy to enjoy from modest sources.
