# Koss ESP/95X

## Summary

The Drop/Massdrop-era version of the Koss electrostatic system. It is sonically close to ESP/950 and can be excellent value, but Vincent's collector preference remains the original ESP/950 when prices are close.

## Status

- Category: Electrostatic headphone system
- Collector tier: S
- Current ownership: Owned / reference flavor
- Priority: Keep as electrostatic reference; upgrade/duplicate only if ESP/950 appears at a strong price

## Version / Identification

- Usually associated with Drop/Massdrop release.
- Darker colorway than original ESP/950.
- Package/accessory set is generally simpler than original ESP/950.

## Sound Signature

### Bass

Fast and clean, but light in physical impact.

### Midrange

Clear, smooth, and open. Very good for vocals, jazz, and relaxed listening.

### Treble

Airy and generally gentle for an electrostatic.

### Soundstage / Imaging

Open and spacious, though not HD800-large.

### Dynamics / Speed

Good microdynamic clarity and speed; limited macrodynamic slam.

## System Synergy

- Provides electrostatic flavor in Vincent's collection.
- Does not replace LCD-X/Pro 900 for aggressive metal or LCD-3 for dense musicality.
- Good reference point when evaluating whether original ESP/950 is worth adding.

## Head-Fi Consensus

### Common Praise

- Excellent value when discounted or bought used.
- Smooth, open electrostatic tone.
- Similar sonic family to ESP/950.

### Common Criticism

- Build still feels inexpensive.
- Energizer limits ultimate performance.
- Not a slam-focused headphone.
- Some older enthusiasts prefer the original ESP/950.

## ESP/95X vs ESP/950 Notes

- Many listeners consider the two extremely close.
- Reported differences may come from pads, production variation, age, and expectation bias.
- Collector status favors ESP/950; value and availability may favor ESP/95X.

## Reliability / Known Issues

- Check channel balance.
- Check for buzzing or stator/driver issues.
- Check E/90 energizer condition.
- Check pad condition and cable strain.
