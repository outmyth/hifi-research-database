# Stax SR-404

## Summary

A later Lambda-family Stax model and a practical vintage/modern bridge electrostatic target. For Vincent, SR-404 is interesting if priced around $450 or below and in clean working condition.

## Status

- Category: Open-back electrostatic earspeaker
- Collector tier: S
- Current ownership: Not owned
- Priority: Opportunistic target at <= $450

## Position in Collection

- More modern/practical than normal-bias Lambda.
- Less ambitious and expensive than SR-007 Mk1.
- Potential upgrade path from Koss ESP/95X if a compatible Stax amp is available.

## Sound Signature

### Bass

Faster and cleaner than dynamic bass, but not planar-slam heavy.

### Midrange

Clear, open, and classic Lambda-family in presentation.

### Treble

Detailed and airy; can be bright depending on chain.

### Soundstage / Imaging

Open Lambda-style staging, good separation.

### Dynamics / Speed

Excellent speed and articulation; limited physical impact compared with Audeze.

## System Synergy

- Requires compatible Stax Pro-bias amplifier/energizer.
- More serious electrostatic path than Koss ESP/95X, but not necessarily better for metal impact.
- Best considered if Vincent wants to expand the electrostatic branch without jumping directly to SR-007.

## Head-Fi Consensus

### Common Praise

- Open, clear Stax sound
- Strong detail and air
- Practical used-market Lambda-family option
- Good value when priced well

### Common Criticism

- Can sound bright/thin with mismatched amps
- Bass impact is limited
- Older units require careful condition checks
- Not as prestigious as SR-007 or older top Stax models

## Reliability / Known Issues

- Check channel balance.
- Check for squeal, imbalance, buzzing, or intermittent signal.
- Check cable and strain relief.
- Pads and headband condition matter.
- Confirm Pro-bias compatibility.

## Buying Guide

### Must Check

- Driver balance
- Cable condition
- Pad/headband condition
- Compatible amp plan
- Seller history

### Nice to Have

- Original box
- Clean pads
- Known working Stax amp pairing

### Avoid

- Untested units
- Channel imbalance
- Damaged cable
- Overpriced units near SR-007 territory
