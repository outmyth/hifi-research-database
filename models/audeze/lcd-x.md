# Audeze LCD-X

## Summary

A faster, more neutral, more studio-oriented Audeze planar than LCD-3. For Vincent, the 2021+ LCD-X is a high-value metal and progressive-metal candidate when priced around $600-$650.

## Status

- Category: Open-back planar magnetic headphone
- Collector tier: SS
- Current ownership: Target / watchlist
- Priority: High at target price

## Preferred Version

- 2021 revision or newer is preferred.
- Earlier LCD-X can still be good, but used value and tuning expectations should be adjusted.

## Sound Signature

### Bass

Tighter and more controlled than LCD-3, with good impact and speed.

### Midrange

More neutral and less lush than LCD-3. Better for clarity, less romantic for vocals.

### Treble

More direct and energetic than LCD-3, generally better for mix detail and attack.

### Soundstage / Imaging

Good planar imaging, less huge than HD800, more grounded and precise.

### Dynamics / Speed

One of its biggest strengths. Better than LCD-3 for aggressive metal precision.

## System Synergy

- Complements HD800 with planar density and stronger bass foundation.
- More redundant with LCD-3 than Pro 900 or ESP/950, but still distinct due to speed/neutrality.
- Best if Vincent wants a metal-focused Audeze rather than a lush classic Audeze.

## Head-Fi Consensus

### Common Praise

- Strong technical performance
- Good bass control
- Excellent for studio and metal use
- More neutral and energetic than LCD-2/LCD-3 family

### Common Criticism

- Can sound less romantic than older Audeze models
- Heavy
- Not as emotionally rich as LCD-3

## Reliability / Known Issues

- Check revision year.
- Check pads and headband.
- Confirm no driver imbalance or buzzing.
- Verify included case/cable.

## Buying Guide

### Must Check

- 2021+ revision confirmation
- Driver balance
- Pad/headband condition
- Case and cable inclusion

### Nice to Have

- Creator package or hard case depending on listing
- Original documentation
- Balanced cable

### Avoid

- Overpriced older revision without clear discount
- Driver issues
- Very worn pads at high price
