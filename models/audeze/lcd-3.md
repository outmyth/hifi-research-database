# Audeze LCD-3

## Summary

A classic warm, dense, musical Audeze planar flagship and one of the highest-priority targets for Vincent's collection, especially in Fazor form with official recent driver replacement.

## Status

- Category: Open-back planar magnetic headphone
- Collector tier: SSS
- Current ownership: Target / watchlist
- Priority: Very high

## Preferred Version

- Fazor version with official Audeze driver replacement or newer driver set.
- 2021/new-driver official service history is especially desirable.
- Carbon fiber headband is a comfort bonus but not the core value driver.

## Versions / Identification

- Pre-Fazor: often valued by some collectors for older Audeze warmth, but higher uncertainty and driver-risk concerns.
- Fazor: more controlled imaging and more predictable modern Audeze ownership experience.
- Official driver-replaced units: highly preferred when documentation is available.

## Sound Signature

### Bass

Full, weighty, and more enveloping than strictly neutral. Less studio-linear than LCD-X, but emotionally satisfying.

### Midrange

The core reason to own LCD-3: dense, rich, organic, and very good for vocals, guitars, and atmospheric rock.

### Treble

Generally smoother rather than sharp. This supports long listening sessions but may not satisfy listeners looking for maximal top-end bite.

### Soundstage / Imaging

More intimate and enveloping than HD800. Not a stage monster, but has strong planar layering.

### Dynamics / Speed

Good planar speed, but the presentation is more lush than aggressive.

## Head-Fi Consensus

### Common Praise

- Lush mids
- Organic tone
- Emotional presentation
- Classic Audeze house sound

### Common Criticism

- Heavy
- Not as fast/neutral as LCD-X or LCD-5 family
- Older units can raise driver-history concerns

## Reliability / Known Issues

- Confirm driver replacement history.
- Check for wood cup cracks.
- Check pads and headband wear.
- Ask about channel balance and any buzzing/rattling.
- Documentation from Audeze service increases value.

## Buying Guide

### Must Check

- Driver revision or service proof
- Wood condition
- Pad condition
- Cable and case inclusion
- Seller reputation

### Nice to Have

- Carbon fiber headband
- Original case
- Recent Audeze service record
- Warranty remaining

### Avoid

- Driver imbalance
- Wood cracks
- Vague service history at premium price
- Overpriced listings above collector value
