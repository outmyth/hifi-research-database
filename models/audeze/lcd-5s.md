# Audeze LCD-5s

## Summary

Audeze's newer flagship direction: more technical, reference-oriented, and modern than LCD-3. For Vincent, LCD-5s is important to track, but it is not the top buy target until used prices mature significantly.

## Status

- Category: Open-back planar magnetic flagship headphone
- Collector tier: S / Watch
- Current ownership: Not owned
- Priority: Research/watch, not immediate buy

## Position in Audeze Lineup

- Newer flagship direction than LCD-3 and LCD-X.
- More reference/monitoring oriented.
- Technical performance is the main attraction.
- Used-market value is not yet mature.

## Sound Signature

### Bass

Expected to be fast, controlled, and cleaner than older lush Audeze models. Less about warmth and bloom than precision.

### Midrange

Likely more neutral and accurate than LCD-3, but less romantic.

### Treble

More revealing and explicit than older Audeze house sound.

### Soundstage / Imaging

Precise, modern planar imaging. Not primarily about HD800-style width.

### Dynamics / Speed

A key advantage over older Audeze models: technical precision, speed, and detail retrieval.
